ABOUT US

1. Registration and Enrollment:

Users visit the platform's website and register for an account. Upon registration, users can browse through the available courses. They can enroll in the courses of their choice, either for free or by purchasing them.

2. Course Structure:

Each course is structured into modules or lessons covering different aspects of blockchain technology. The course material includes text-based content, videos, interactive quizzes, assignments, and projects. Courses may offer different levels of difficulty, catering to beginners, intermediate, and advanced learners. .

3. Learning Experience:

Students progress through the course material at their own pace. They can access the course content anytime, anywhere, using their computers or mobile devices. Interactive features such as quizzes, assignments, and discussion forums engage students and reinforce learning.
